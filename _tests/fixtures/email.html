VI. Old Business
1. Reserve Study
Dennis provided updated information to the reserve study discussion from last meeting: The full quote from Reserve Advisors was $1850, $925 down payment and an additional $925 upon completion. They will send one or more engineers to the property to gather data and do the calculations for what our status is and what our exposure is. They will give us a report, what-if scenarios, and will meet with the neighborhood to review the report. There was a discussion about the purpose and it was decided that it is needed to have information to enable prevention rather than being put in a position of crisis management. A motion was made to spend $1,850 to retain Reserve Advisors to do a reserve study. The motion was seconded and approved unanimously.
