Alameda Ward Primary Newsletter
October 2015
 
Dear Families,

    Happy October! We are so grateful for your kids! They all did a marvelous job in the primary program. The Spirit was so strong and we have heard nothing but genuine praise for these fabulous children. Thank you for raising such sweet children! We want to give a shout out to our incredible primary teachers, music leader and primary pianist for all their help and patience.

    We are having another primary bonfire in November! We had so much fun and felt this was a great way to "hasten the work" that we are doing it again. It will be November 13th at the Griffin's house. We encourage you to have your kids invite their friends. Thank you for all you do! We love interacting with your children!
Sincerely, 
Your Primary Presidency
