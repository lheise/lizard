Alameda Ward Primary Newsletter
April 2016
 
Dear Families,

    Happy April! Hope you all enjoyed conference! It was so uplifting! Last month we focused a lot on following the Prophet. It was awesome that we had conference right after. This month's theme is Jesus Christ is my Savior and Redeemer. We want the children to know that they have a Savior who loves them and will help them in their life.

    Our popcorn challenge is going well. Just a reminder- each child can earn up to 14 popcorn kernels each week if they read by themselves and with their family. It does not have to be long. Whatever works for your family is great.

    We will be having a primary activity Saturday, May 7th at the church from 9-10:30am. This will be for all junior and senior primary children. The theme will be Scripture Power! We will learn the primary song and make superhero capes. We will also produce a mini-music video together. Some very special people will come and dress up as superheroes from the scriptures. Desserts worthy of a superhero will be there as well!
Sincerely, 
Your Primary Presidency
