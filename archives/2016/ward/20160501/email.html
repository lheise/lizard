May 2016
 
May 1, 2016

Dear member of the Alameda Ward family,

    The bishopric invites you to open the second issue of the “Alameda Ward Newsletter.”

    We have included updates for births, sustainings, and move-ins during the month of April, plus a calendar of upcoming events just for the month of May.

    You will find a few photos and descriptions of the month’s events, including the Relief Society’s mid-week activity. Thanks to Inge-Lise and Becca Jo for providing the information. Without their help members of the priesthood would remain largely ignorant of just what goes on when the Relief Society gets together.

    We are proud of the ward missionaries, especially Garrett Bedford and the sister missionaries, for organizing and executing a fine story-telling social. You’ll find a few photos of the event, along with summaries of the stories. As a bishopric we place great emphasis on family story-telling. What child doesn’t like to hear stories of Uncle Joe or Aunt Mary, or pioneer ancestors, family reunions, how Mom and Dad met?

    We have dedicated a full two-page spread to our dear Louise Bushman, who passed away during the month of April. Her funeral service on April 30th was truly uplifting.

    Save the newsletter to your hard drive for safekeeping. Once downloaded, the best way to view the document is to press “View” at the top of the page, hover over “Page Display” and click on “Two-Up.” It should then read (and print) like a book.
Sincerely, 
Bishop Baldo Besich
1st Counselor Eric Thornhill
2nd Counselor Dwight Davis
