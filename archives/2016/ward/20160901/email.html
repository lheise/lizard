Dear member of the Alameda Ward family,

    We hope you enjoy the August issue of the Alameda Ward Newsletter. We’ve highlighted some of the month’s social activities, provided calendar items, and included highlights from talks given in our ward and in our stake’s annual general priesthood meeting. Don’t miss tips for emergency preparedness. There is even a scripture crossword puzzle for kids.

    Save the newsletter to your hard drive for safekeeping. Once downloaded, the best way to view the document is to press “View” at the top of the page, hover over “Page Display” and click on “Two-Up.” It should then read (and print) like a book.
