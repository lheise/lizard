November 1, 2016

Dear member of the Alameda Ward family,

    We hope you enjoy the October issue of the Alameda Ward Newsletter. We’ve highlighted our new missionary, Sister Jun, and have provided a photo review of the October Cub Pack Meeting and the Halloween party.

    Be sure to read Richard Heywood’s August 28th talk on patriarchal blessings, excerpts from William Bushman’s October 23rd talk about rescuing/supporting lost souls, and highlights from Mary Bosen’s October 16th talk on gospel study.

    Larry Faas lifted Orson Pratt’s “27 Rules of Celestial Marriage” from the Mormon Chronicles website and condensed them into language common to the age in which we now live. To see the original document, go to http://www.mormonchronicle.com/27-rules-of-celestial-marriage.

    You will find Jeanne Taysom’s new address on page 16, along with a calendar of upcoming events. Succeeding pages include Jerry Ford’s letter to Sunday School teachers, Thanksgiving thoughts, and Lincoln’s 1863 Thanksgiving Day Proclamation.

    We are delighted that Patricia Engler was baptized on October 29th, and welcome her to the Alameda Ward.

    Don’t forget the crossword puzzle on the final page.

    Save the newsletter to your hard drive for safekeeping. Once downloaded, the best way to view the document is to press “View” at the top of the page, hover over “Page Display” and click on “Two-Up” and “Show Cover Page During Two-Up.” It should then read (and print) like a book.
Sincerely, 
Bishop Baldo Besich
1st Counselor Eric Thornhill
