Alameda Ward Newsletter February 2017
March 1, 2017

Dear member of the Alameda Ward family,

          We hope you enjoy the February issue of the Alameda Ward Newsletter. We draw your attention to the front-page ad for TEMPE CARES. There is still time to sign up.

          Photos of the Ward Picnic follow on pages 2 and 3. The Relief Society chose February 25th to a service project, Produce On Wheels With Out Waste (P.O.W.W.O.W.). See pages 4 and 5.

          A calendar of upcoming events and various announcements occupy page 6, followed by a spotlight of the Hepworth family on page 7. This spotlight is a reprint of similar content in one of our Sunday bulletins. We appreciate Cindy Bowles for putting these spotlights together.

          We hope all of our readers will take a hard look at pages 8 and 9, where we present a family history application called Relative Finder. Not only is this a fun personal and family exercise, it can serve as a Segway into descendancy research. Be sure to join the “Tempe Arizona Stake” group to discover your relationship to members of the stake. You may be surprised by the results!

          Lastly, try the “Book of Mormon Crossword” puzzle on the last page. This could make for a good family home evening exercise.

          Save the newsletter to your hard drive for safekeeping. Once downloaded, the best way to view the document is to press “View” at the top of the page, hover over “Page Display” and click on “Two-Up” and “Show Cover Page During Two-Up.” It should then read (and print) like a book.
Sincerely, 
Bishop Baldo Besich
1st Counselor Dwight Davis
2nd Counselor Moriah Hepworth
