Alameda Ward Newsletter April 2017
May 1, 2017

Dear member of the Alameda Ward family,

          We have attached the April issue of the Alameda Ward Newsletter. The front page contains a partial re-print of a New York Times article about ways to “develop a strong family narrative.” We hope you enjoy it and look forward to our annual story-telling fireside in September.

          We mourn the passing of Dan Wilhelm. He was so efficient and pleasant in the calendaring of stake-wide events.

          The Ellis Island Family Discovery Day is highlighted on pages 6 and 7, followed by interesting Church survey returns on the following two pages. Yes, family history is huge – so many facets to this important work.

          Cindy Bowles does such a fine job generating “Spotlights” that feature members of the ward. A reprint of the Brandt and Bethany Platt spotlight comprises page 10.

          The Easter Program narration is presented on pages 12 and 13. We thank Lauren Besich for a fine job matching text with music.

          Erin McDowell and Emily Coons gave wonderful talks on the 23rd of April. Excerpts from each talk are presented on pages 16 and 17.

          Finally, the puzzle. This one tests your knowledge of Abraham. Have fun with it.

          Save the newsletter to your hard drive for safekeeping. Once downloaded, the best way to view the document is to press “View” at the top of the page, hover over “Page Display” and click on “Two-Up” and “Show Cover Page During Two-Up.” It should then read (and print) like a book.
Sincerely, 
Bishop Baldo Besich
