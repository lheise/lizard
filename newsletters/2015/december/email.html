Alameda Ward Primary Newsletter
December 2015
 
Dear Families,

    Merry Christmas! We love this time of year! Last Sunday in primary, the kids made mangers and were asked to fill the manager with "hay" by doing kind things for other people. They will be given a baby Jesus ornament on December 20th. We hope they will spend this season serving others so they can find the true joy of this holiday season.

    The children will be singing in the Ward Christmas party this Friday, December 4th. It will be so fun! Be sure to bring your families and friends. The kids will also be singing in church on Sunday, December 13th. Their sweet voices bring such a sweet spirit.

    We hope you have a fabulous Christmas season!
Sincerely, 
Your Primary Presidency
