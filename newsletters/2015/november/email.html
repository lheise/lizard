Alameda Ward Primary Newsletter
November 2015
 
Dear Families,

    Wow! November! As we tend to think about this month as the "thankful" month, we want you to know how thankful we are for your kids! We love them and their sweet spirits!

    This month the themes is: "When We Serve Others, We Serve God". We gave the children a challenge to do secret service. We gave them cards with smiley faces. They are to do service for someone in the family and then leave the smiley face. Please encourage them to do this so they can tell us when we meet again on the 15th.

    We are super excited for our Primary Activity on November 13th from 6-7:15. The party is at the Griffin's home 511 E. Geneva Dr. Tempe, AZ 85282. We will be roasting hot dogs and making "woofems" and smores. This was so fun last year! We have challenged the kids to invite a friend. We gave them fliers in primary. Parents are welcome to come and make a "woofem" but not required. We will have lots of adult supervision so whatever you feel comfortable with.
Sincerely, 
Your Primary Presidency
