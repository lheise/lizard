Dear Families,

    We are so excited for the primary program on September 20th. Please invite your family and friends to attend, as the primary program is always a special one! You may download a pdf of the primary program here. Please practice with your child and if possible, please have them memorize their part. If they have a part where they need to write their own experience, please have the part written out and brought with them to church on Sunday, September 6th. We will be practicing September 6th and 13th. If you have any questions, please call Sister Lewis at (480) 353-1698. Thank you for all your help with this! We love the primary program and know it will be a great spiritual experience for your child and our ward family!
Sincerely, 
Your Primary Presidency
