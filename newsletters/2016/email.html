Alameda Ward Primary Newsletter
March 2016
 
Dear Families,

    Happy March! It's Easter this month! We encourage you to take your families and non-member friends to see the Mesa Easter Pageant. It is March 16th - 18th and March 22nd - 26th in English and March 19th in Spanish. It is held on the temple grounds and begins at 8pm and is 65 minutes long. It is a beautiful pageant and reminds us the reason we celebrate Easter.

    As you know the theme for this year is "I Know the Scriptures are True." One of the main things we want to focus on is having the kids read the scriptures daily. On Sunday, we will be introducing an incentive program to get the kids excited to read. Here are two different scripture trackers. This chart is more like a coloring page and this one is a daily tracker. We will compare reading the scriptures to popcorn. Each time a child reads the scriptures they can put a popcorn kernel in a jar and when the jar is full we will have a popcorn party at the end of sharing time. They get a kernel each day they read. If they read by themselves and with their families they get 2. They can earn up to 14 kernels a week. We want the kids to understand and gain a testimony of how important the scriptures are. Please encourage your kids to read. There is no set time limit- just whatever works for your family and child.
Sincerely, 
Your Primary Presidency
