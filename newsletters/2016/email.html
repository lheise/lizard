Alameda Ward Primary Newsletter
January 2016
 
Dear Families,

    Wow! It's 2016! We are so excited for a new year in primary. This year's theme is "I Know the Scriptures are True". We look forward to increasing your child's love and testimony of the scriptures this year.

    With a new year comes new classes. You can download a map, schedule, list of classes, and a Book of Mormon reading chart here. If you have any questions please feel free to call any of the presidency. We are excited to see you on Sunday! Just a reminder sacrament meeting starts at 12:30.

    The General primary presidency has asked us to give the children opportunities to share what they have learned from their scripture reading. We will be incorporating this into our weekly sharing time. This will give the children a chance to share their experiences and help motivate others to read their scriptures at home. If your child doesn't already read the scriptures by themselves or with their family, we encourage this to be one of their goals for the new year.
Sincerely, 
Your Primary Presidency
