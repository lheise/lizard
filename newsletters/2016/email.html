Alameda Ward Primary Newsletter
February 2016
 
Dear Families,

    Happy February!! With this month being known as the love month we just want you to know how much we love the Alameda primary children. We feel so blessed to see them each Sunday and feel of their sweet spirits.

    We have encouraged senior primary to read the Book of Mormon this year. We also encourage the families of junior primary to read the scriptures daily with their children. We have several extra print outs of Book of Mormon reading charts available, so if your child would like to have one, just stop by after church and we will give you one.

    Recently, we have had lots of changes in primary. We are very thankful for the service of Sister Larson and Sister Davis. They will be missed, but we are excited for their new opportunities to serve jn other areas of the ward. We welcome Sister McDowell as 1st counselor, Sister Vicino as 2nd counselor, and Sister Nielson as the primary chorister. We are also excited to have Brother and Sister De Paula and Brother and Sister Sandoval join us as teachers. We are excited to have the children get to know them better.
Sincerely, 
Your Primary Presidency
