Alameda Ward Newsletter
June 2016
 
July 1, 2016

Dear member of the Alameda Ward family,

    The June issue contains the full texts of talks given by President Johnson and High Counselor Mike Ostler at the recent Stake Conference. We also highlight a ward event and share news from the general authorities regarding the Sunday School program. We have included a tribute to Rolanda Santiago-Cruz who tragically died this month, and a thank-you letter Ann Bushman Chambers wrote to the Alameda Ward on behalf of her siblings. Page 13 contains a link to an amusing youtube song entitled, “I’m My Own Grampa.” It’s worth the listening. Some of Addison Graham’s beautiful artwork is displayed on page 7.

    Save the newsletter to your hard drive for safekeeping. Once downloaded, the best way to view the document is to press “View” at the top of the page, hover over “Page Display” and click on “Two-Up.” It should then read (and print) like a book.
Sincerely, 
Bishop Baldo Besich
1st Counselor Eric Thornhill
2nd Counselor Dwight Davis
