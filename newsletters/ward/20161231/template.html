January 1, 2017

Dear member of the Alameda Ward family,

          We hope you enjoy the December stand-alone issue of the Alameda Ward Newsletter. The month of December began with the Young Women in Excellence “Night of Celebration” dinner at Shirley Snedigar’s beautifully decorated home. Shirley baked five pies. What more can one say?!!! The evening was capped with the release of lanterns into the night sky. You’ll see some photos at the bottom of the front page.

          You’ll find lots of photos from the Ward’s “Feliz Navidad” Christmas Party. Thanks to the Primary presidency for making this party a great success.

          The complete transcript of Moriah Hepworth’s December 11th talk on Miracles begins on page 11. Great reading, we promise.

          Page 13 contains an important message concerning temple ordinance reservations. The Church is now implementing its policy of releasing reserved names after a holding period of two years.

          Kirsten Davis married Raymond Pyle in the Phoenix Arizona Temple on the 27th of December. Three days later Carly Thornhill married Taylor Davison in the Mesa Arizona Temple. See page 14.

          Don’t forget to print and attempt the crossword puzzle on page 18, and do not look at page 20 until you’ve gone as far as you can.

          Save the newsletter to your hard drive for safekeeping. Once downloaded, the best way to view the document is to press “View” at the top of the page, hover over “Page Display” and click on “Two-Up” and “Show Cover Page During Two-Up.” It should then read (and print) like a book.
Sincerely, 
Bishop Baldo Besich
1st Counselor Eric Thornhill
