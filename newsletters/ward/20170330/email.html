Alameda Ward Newsletter March 2017
April 1, 2017

Dear member of the Alameda Ward family,

          We hope you enjoy the March issue of the Alameda Ward Newsletter. The front page contains a message from the bishopric urging us to broaden our horizons, seek understanding, love our neighbors, enlighten ourselves by reading good books, listening to uplifting music, engaging with members of the community, and reflecting on the meaning of the Easter season.

          Page 3 invites us to attend the annual Chili Cookoff and Dessert Auction on the 8th of April. Perhaps you will want to make some Chili for others to enjoy, or create a tasty dessert for the raffle. Proceeds benefit our own Scout Troop 74.

          Page 16 celebrates the baptisms of Justin Pinnt and Chester Thomas, and highlights Sister Cottrell who joined us as Sister Lott’s companion at the beginning of the month.

          The Primary children were treated to an interview with the prophet Nephi. It was brief but packed. See Page 17.

          Page 19 gives an overview of upcoming lessons and significant events in the ward organization.

          Don’t overlook the Easter puzzle on Page 20. It has the makings of a family home evening.

          Save the newsletter to your hard drive for safekeeping. Once downloaded, the best way to view the document is to press “View” at the top of the page, hover over “Page Display” and click on “Two-Up” and “Show Cover Page During Two-Up.” It should then read (and print) like a book.
Sincerely, 
Bishop Baldo Besich
1st Counselor Dwight Davis
2nd Counselor Moriah Hepworth
